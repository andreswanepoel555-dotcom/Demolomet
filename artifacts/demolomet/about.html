Who We Are

The Demolomet Story

Demolomet was founded on a simple principle: do the hard work right, every time. Based in the North West province of South Africa, we've grown from a small local operation into one of the most reliable demolition and scrap metal companies in the region.

Over 15 years in this industry has taught us that every project — from a small clearance to a full industrial demolition — deserves the same level of care, professionalism, and commitment to safety. We don't cut corners. We don't take shortcuts. We finish what we start.

Our team is made up of experienced operators, safety-conscious site managers, and a crew that takes real pride in their work. When you call Demolomet, you're not just getting a contractor — you're getting a partner who will see your project through to the end.
