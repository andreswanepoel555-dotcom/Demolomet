What We Offer

Industrial Services

From full-scale demolition to responsible scrap metal recovery — we handle the heavy work so you don't have to.

Service 01

Demolition Services

Expert demolition for commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and large-scale concrete structures. We use heavy machinery and controlled techniques to bring structures down safely, efficiently, and with minimal disruption. Our team manages every phase — from planning and permits to final execution.

  • Commercial & residential building demolition
  • Industrial facility teardown
  • Concrete crushing and removal
  • Controlled mechanical demolition
  • Hazardous material handling
  • Rubble removal and disposal
Service 02

Scrap Metal Collection

We collect, sort, and responsibly dispose of all types of scrap metal — from construction offcuts to full industrial equipment. Whether you're clearing a factory floor or wrapping up a building project, Demolomet makes scrap metal removal fast, fair, and hassle-free.

  • Ferrous and non-ferrous metal collection
  • Factory and warehouse cleanouts
  • Construction site scrap removal
  • Copper, aluminium, and steel recovery
  • Scheduled pickups available
  • On-the-spot weighing and valuation
Service 03

Site Clearing

Preparing a plot for new construction? Our site clearing service removes all existing structures, vegetation, debris, and rubble to leave you with a clean, level surface ready for your next development. We work quickly and leave sites safe and accessible.

  • Full plot clearing and levelling
  • Vegetation and tree removal
  • Existing structure demolition
  • Debris and rubble hauling
  • Soil preparation for development
  • Commercial and agricultural sites
Service 04

Industrial Cleanup

Large-scale industrial cleanups require experience, the right equipment, and a team that doesn't cut corners. Demolomet handles post-demolition cleanup, factory decommissioning, and industrial waste removal — leaving sites clean, compliant, and ready for what comes next.

  • Post-demolition debris removal
  • Factory and plant decommissioning
  • Industrial waste sorting and disposal
  • Oil and chemical spill site prep
  • Asbestos management coordination
  • Final site inspection and sign-off
